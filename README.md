# Hotel Booking Demand: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Project Overview
This project performs an end-to-end Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on hotel booking data
to understand booking patterns, guest behavior, pricing trends, seasonality,
and cancellation drivers across City Hotel and Resort Hotel.

## Business Objectives
- Identify which hotel type performs better in terms of demand and pricing
- Analyze seasonal booking and cancellation trends
- Understand guest preferences and market segments
- Study cancellation behavior and its drivers

## Dataset
- Dataset Name: Hotel Booking Demand
- File: hotel_bookings.csv
- Source: Kaggle

## Key Insights
- City Hotels receive higher booking volume compared to Resort Hotels.
- Resort Hotels show longer average length of stay.
- Average Daily Rate (ADR) peaks during summer months (July–August).
- Bed & Breakfast (BB) is the most preferred meal type.
- Guests with higher special requests tend to cancel less.
- Online TA is the dominant market segment.
- Cancellations are higher in City Hotels and during certain peak months.
